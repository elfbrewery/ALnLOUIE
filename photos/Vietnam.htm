Photo Album of Trip to Vietnam, September, 2016

We visited Vietnam on a 12 day tour. We didn't go individually this time, so it was interesting to see the difference an organized tour could make.
Three days of flying; jet lag; 9 busy days of touring by bus, boat, you name it; heat plus humidity; and being pampered in 5 star hotels. Totally out of our elements. We didn't include a lot of typical shots of temples, pagodas and tombs; there are plenty of these on the internet.
