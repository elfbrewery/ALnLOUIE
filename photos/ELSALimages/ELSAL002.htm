The Devil's Door, a promitory of rock that lost it's middle making it resemble two horns. Volcanoes are everywhere. A lake nearby is the crater of the Ilopango volcano that spewed ash into the sky and blocked the sun around the world for 18 months back in 535-6(?) A.D.
