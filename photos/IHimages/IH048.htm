Bonifacio on the Hill [Corsica]
Back to Photo Album              Photo Advance: Previous        Next

The old part of touristy Bonifacio lies within the walls up on the hill. The new part is downhill and along the coast. It's a slog up to the town and there is a tourist train to take one there, but we saw the best parts from the sea (and I think the train runs only in summer, this is May). Off we go to Porto Vecchio by bus.
