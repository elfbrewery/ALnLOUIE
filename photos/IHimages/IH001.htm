We started in Valetta on Malta for a week. After seeing Malta and Gozo we flew to Cagliari, Sardinia. About a week later we toke the one hour ferry to Bonifacio, Corsica. Our last stop in Corsica was Bastia from where we took a big overnight ferry to Marseille, France and then flew home to British Columbia.
