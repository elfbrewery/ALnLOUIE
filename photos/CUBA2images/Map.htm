We flew into Havana (1 night) and then flew from Havana to Baracoa (for 4 days), then took the bus to Santiago, passing Guantanamo Bay (spent 2 days),
then took an overnight bus to Trinidad (for 2 days) and by bus and private taxi to Playa Larga (for 2 weeks), finally a private taxi back to Havana (1 night).
